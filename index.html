Life is a constant process of learning. I imagine people in an endless library; each, having their book basket, collects books and experiences. Even though our book basket, our time of life, is limited, this fact is the reason why every basket is different. This gives me a great joy. In an eternal library, we are all some different collections of information.

 I am a M.Sc student, research and teaching assistant  in the Department of Industrial Engineering at Boğaziçi University, Istanbul, Turkey. I obtained my B.Sc degree from the same department. I am also a member of Socio-Economic System Dynamics Research Group.  Currently, I am preparing to move to Melbourne to study as a PhD student in the School of Computing and Information Systems in UniMelb.

My research areas include Causality Estimation from Data, Systems Theory, Dynamic Health Systems, Epigenetics, System Dynamics, Policy Analysis on Socio-Economic Systems, Data Mining. Yet, one cannot be described by only their academic research areas. I am interested in visual arts, especially paintings. You can see some paintings of mine from this link.
